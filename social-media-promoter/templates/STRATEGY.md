# Social Media Promotion Strategy

## 1. Optimal Posting Times by Platform

All times reflect peak engagement windows based on platform demographics. Adjust for your primary audience timezone.

| Platform | Best Times (Local TZ) | Best Days | Timezone Notes |
|----------|----------------------|-----------|----------------|
| X (Twitter) | 8-10 AM, 12-1 PM, 5-6 PM | Tue, Wed, Thu | Use ET for US tech audience. For global reach, 9 AM ET covers EU afternoon + US morning. |
| Discord | 10 AM - 12 PM, 7-9 PM | Weekdays for work tools, weekends for consumer | Match the server's most active timezone. Check server insights if available. |
| Xiaohongshu | 7-9 AM, 12-2 PM, 8-10 PM | Tue-Thu, Sat-Sun | CST (UTC+8). Evening posts perform best for tech content. |
| Reddit | 6-9 AM ET | Mon, Tue, Wed | Early morning ET catches both US early risers and EU afternoon. Post timing matters more on Reddit than other platforms. |
| Product Hunt | 12:01 AM PT (launch) | Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday | Always PT. Avoid Mondays (competition from weekend backlog) and Fridays (lower traffic). |
| Zhihu | 8-10 PM | Weekdays | CST (UTC+8). Evening hours when professionals browse after work. Weekend mornings also perform well. |

## 2. Content Adaptation Rules

### English Platforms (X, Discord, Reddit, Product Hunt)

- **Tone**: Direct, concise, value-first. Avoid corporate-speak.
- **Structure**: Lead with the hook or value proposition. Details follow.
- **Length**: Shorter is better on X. Medium on Discord/Product Hunt. Reddit rewards depth.
- **Social proof**: Metrics, testimonials, GitHub stars, user counts.
- **CTA style**: Clear and single-action ("Try it free", "Check it out").

### Chinese Platforms (Xiaohongshu, Zhihu)

- **Do NOT simply translate English content.** Rewrite from scratch for each platform's culture.
- **Xiaohongshu**: Personal recommendation tone. Heavy emoji usage. Visual-first. Frame tech products as lifestyle/productivity upgrades. Use trending slang naturally.
- **Zhihu**: Academic/professional tone. Deep technical analysis. Show original thinking. Product mentions must be earned through valuable content. Superficial posts get downvoted.
- **Cultural nuances**:
  - Reference local competitors and ecosystem (e.g., compare to Chinese tools, not just US ones)
  - Use Chinese tech community terminology naturally
  - Pricing should mention CNY or provide context for USD pricing
  - Avoid politically sensitive comparisons or references

### Cross-Platform Content Matrix

| Content Element | X | Discord | Xiaohongshu | Reddit | Product Hunt | Zhihu |
|----------------|---|---------|-------------|--------|-------------|-------|
| Product name mention | Prominent | Prominent | Natural/subtle | Subtle | Prominent | Subtle |

## 3. Engagement Follow-Up Guidelines

### Response Timing

- **First 2 hours after posting**: Respond to every comment within 10-15 minutes. This is the critical engagement window that signals activity to platform algorithms.
- **Hours 2-24**: Respond within 1 hour during active hours.
- **After 24 hours**: Respond within same day.

### Response Strategy by Comment Type

**Questions about features/functionality:**
- Answer directly and concisely
- Link to relevant docs if the answer is complex
- If the feature doesn't exist, acknowledge and note it as feedback

**Positive feedback:**
- Thank them specifically (reference what they said)
- Ask a follow-up question to deepen engagement
- Never respond with just "Thanks!"

**Criticism or negative feedback:**
- Acknowledge the concern without being defensive
- Explain your reasoning if relevant, but don't argue
- If the criticism is valid, say so and explain your plan to address it

**"How is this different from X?" comparisons:**
- Be respectful of competitors
- Focus on your unique approach, not competitor weaknesses
- Be specific and honest about tradeoffs

### Platform-Specific Engagement

- **Reddit**: Upvote helpful replies. Respond to top-level comments first. Never ask for upvotes.
- **Product Hunt**: Reply to every single comment on launch day. The maker's engagement rate directly impacts ranking.
- **Xiaohongshu**: Reply with emojis and casual tone. Pin the most useful comment. Respond to "how to use" questions with mini-tutorials.
- **Zhihu**: Add depth in comment replies. Reference sources. Treat comments as an extension of the article.

## 4. A/B Testing Approach

### What to Test

| Element | How to Test | Minimum Sample |
|---------|-------------|----------------|
| **Headlines/Hooks** | Post variations 24+ hours apart on same platform, or test across comparable subreddits | 2-3 variants |
| **CTA phrasing** | Alternate between posts over a 2-week cycle | 4+ posts per variant |
| **Posting time** | Same content type at different times across weeks | 3+ data points per time slot |
| **Content format** | Thread vs single tweet, long vs short Reddit post | 3+ posts per format |
| **Visual style** | Different cover images/thumbnails on Xiaohongshu | 3+ posts per style |

### Testing Process

1. **Isolate one variable at a time.** Never change headline AND posting time simultaneously.
2. **Track metrics per platform:**
   - X: Impressions, thread read-through rate, link clicks, replies
   - Discord: Reactions, thread replies, link clicks
   - Xiaohongshu: Views, saves (collections), comments, shares
   - Reddit: Upvote ratio, comment count, link clicks
   - Product Hunt: Upvotes, comments, rank position
   - Zhihu: Views, upvotes, collections, comment quality
3. **Record results** in a structured format for the AI to learn from in future iterations.
4. **Minimum test duration**: 2 weeks per variable to account for day-of-week variance.

### Headline Patterns to Test

- Question format: "Why do teams still do X manually?"
- How-to format: "How to reduce code review time by 90%"
- Announcement format: "Introducing ProductName: one-line description"
- Contrarian format: "Code review is broken. Here's the data."
- Story format: "I spent 6 months building a tool to fix my biggest frustration"

## 5. Campaign Cadence Recommendations

### Launch Campaign (Week 1-2)

| Day | Platform | Content Type |
|-----|----------|-------------|
| Day 1 (Tue-Thu) | Product Hunt | Full launch (12:01 AM PT) |
| Day 1 | X | Launch announcement thread |
| Day 1 | Discord | Announcement in relevant servers (stagger, don't spam) |
| Day 2 | Reddit | Show HN / r/SideProject post |
| Day 3 | Zhihu | Technical deep-dive article |
| Day 4 | Xiaohongshu | Visual product intro post |
| Day 5 | Reddit | Value-first post in niche subreddit |
| Week 2 | X | Results/metrics thread ("One week since launch, here's what happened") |
| Week 2 | Zhihu | Follow-up answering popular questions |

### Ongoing Cadence (Post-Launch)

| Platform | Frequency | Content Focus |
|----------|-----------|---------------|
| X | 3-4 posts/week | Tips, updates, user stories, engagement replies |
| Discord | 1-2/week | Feature updates, community Q&A, feedback requests |
| Xiaohongshu | 2-3/week | Use case stories, tips, visual tutorials |
| Reddit | 1-2/month | Value-first posts, helpful comments in relevant threads |
| Product Hunt | Per major release | New launches for significant updates only |
| Zhihu | 2-4/month | Technical articles, answer trending questions |

### Content Recycling Rules

- A single product update can generate content for all 6 platforms, but each must be **rewritten natively** for the platform, not copy-pasted.
- Wait at least 24 hours between posting the same underlying topic on different platforms.
- Reddit and Zhihu content should have the most original analysis; X and Xiaohongshu can be lighter adaptations.
- Track which angles perform best on each platform and double down on those.

### Scaling Signals

Increase posting frequency when:
- Engagement rate is above platform average (varies by platform)
- Comment sentiment is positive and generating organic discussion
- Follower/subscriber growth is accelerating

Decrease or pause when:
- Engagement drops below 50% of your baseline
- Comments become negative about frequency ("this feels like spam")
- You're reusing content without enough differentiation
